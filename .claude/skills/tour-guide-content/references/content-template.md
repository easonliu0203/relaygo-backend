```markdown
<!-- Hook: convince the reader this route is worth their time -->
## Why This Route?

[2-3 sentences. Answer: "What makes this unique?" and "Who is this for?"]
[Example: families, first-timers, photographers, foodies]

<!-- Main body: each attraction gets its own section -->
## Highlights

### [emoji] [Attraction Name] — [Catchy Subtitle]
<!-- Emoji: use a relevant one (🏮🌊⛰️🎆💧🌲🏖️🏯♨️) -->
<!-- Subtitle: short, evocative phrase that captures the essence -->

<!-- Alt text should describe the scene for accessibility & SEO -->
<!-- Photo MUST be verified (HTTP 200) and location-relevant -->

[Paragraph 1: What you'll see/experience. Paint the scene vividly.]
[Paragraph 2: History, cultural context, or fun facts.]
[Paragraph 3: (optional) Seasonal variations or insider knowledge.]

**Must-Eat:** <!-- Only for locations with notable food -->
- **[Shop Name]** — [Signature item + what makes it special]
```

| Language | Target | Range |

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Generic descriptions** — "Beautiful scenery" says nothing. Describe the specific view.
2. **Unrealistic timing** — Don't plan 6 stops in 8 hours with 30-min drives between each.
3. **Missing seasonal context** — "Best in autumn" without saying why or what months.
4. **Food without names** — "Try local snacks" is useless. Name the shop and dish.
5. **Wrong photos** — A Hualien coast photo in a Jiufen guide. Always verify location.
